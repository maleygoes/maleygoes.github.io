Faculty Development Application

An application builder for conference travel and tuition reimbursement.

This page lets you build either a conference travel packet or a tuition reimbursement packet, then generates a completed PDF application for you to forward to your department chair, and then the committee for review. Please note: For all cost estimates, documentation must be provided.

  • Conference travel or tuition reimbursement
  • Shared funding and approval workflow
  • Rationale and anticipated outcomes
  • Supporting files appended to the PDF
  • Fillable signature fields
