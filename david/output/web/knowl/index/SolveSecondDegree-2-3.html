We could undo the squaring of \(x\) by taking the square root of both sides. There is, though, a wrinkle in this approach. There are actually two solutions to this equation: \(x\) could be \(5\) since \(5^2=25\text{,}\) but \(x\) could also be \(-5\) because \((-5)^2=25\) as well.
